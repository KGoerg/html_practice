I Love My Family

Thankfulness

I want to spend some time explaining why I'm thankful for each of my family members. It's important to stop, reflect, and be grateful. We've had a lot of ups and downs and now, things are peaceful, so there's plenty to be thankful for!

I'm very fortunate to have a loving family consisting of my husband, Aaron, and my two cats, Gaia and Hot Sauce. Gaia and Hot Sauce are two one-and-a-half year old cats who keep me on my toes and help make our household a joy to live in!

Aaron

Aaron is a wonderful person who makes me laugh every day and makes me feel so loved. I've known him since 2015, when we first met at Illinois State University shortly before our freshman year ended. I thought he seemed nice and I could tell he was talented -- he was making his own music and showed off his creativity during our Dungeons & Dragons session, which is where I first laid eyes on him. I knew I wanted to get to know him better, and coincidentally, he grew up in a city half an hour away from where I grew up, and we were heading to our parents' homes for the summer, so we were able to see each other very easily for a couple of months before we started back at ISU!
